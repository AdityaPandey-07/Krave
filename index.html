Our Story

Founded in 2020, Krave Momos began as a small street food stall in Prayagraj with a passion for creating the most flavorful momos in town. What started as a humble venture soon became the talk of the city.

Today, we take pride in serving authentic momos recipes passed down through generations, combined with innovative twists that keep our customers coming back for more.

Every momo is handcrafted with care, using only the freshest ingredients and our secret spice blends that make our flavors truly unique.
